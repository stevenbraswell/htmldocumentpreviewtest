Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the TP31 receipt printer.

  1. Using T/SOP, perform the module shut-down procedure for the receipt printer (refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  2. Open the rear chassis door.
  3. Unlatch the green plunger and completely extend the component tray.
  4. Remove the paper from the printer.
  5. Disconnect the releasable tie wrap that holds the cables to the printer.
  6. Disconnect the USB and power cables from the printer (TP31 Receipt Printer Cables).
  7. Loosen the two mounting screws in the keyhole slots at the rear of the printer (TP31 Receipt Printer Mounting Screws).
  8. Remove the printer from the system.
