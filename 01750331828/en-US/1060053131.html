Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the environmental /security camera. Diebold Nixdorf only provides the mounting bracket for the environmental /security camera. The camera is the customer's responsibility.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Perform the T/SOP module shut-down procedure for the environmental / security camera (refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  3. Disconnect the USB cable from the camera.
  4. Remove the three screws that secures the camera bracket to the fascia (Removing and Replacing the Environmental / Security Camera Bracket).
  5. Remove the camera and bracket from the fascia.
  6. Remove one screw to remove the camera from the bracket.
