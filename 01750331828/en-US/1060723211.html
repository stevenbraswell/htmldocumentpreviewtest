Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the four coin dispenser hoppers.

NOTICE

To avoid confusion, only remove one coin hopper at a time. Placement of the hoppers is important. A specific denomination has a specific location.

  1. Open the upper chassis rear door.
  2. Using T/SOP, perform procedure to place the system in the maintenance mode. Refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up.
  3. Release the latch on the coin module slides (Coin Module Main Tray ).
  4. Using the green handle, pull out the main tray of the coin dispenser to the fully extended position.
  5. Unlock the coin hopper cover and lift up the cover.

  10. Remove the screws at both ends of the coin hopper shaft (Removing the Coin Hopper Shaft ).
  11. Remove the coin hopper shaft that runs through all of the coin hoppers (Removing the Coin Hopper Shaft ).
  12. Remove the coin hopper(s).
