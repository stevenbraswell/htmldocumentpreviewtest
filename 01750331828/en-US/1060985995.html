Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the chassis interface board.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slides.
  2. Using T/SOP, perform the system shutdown procedure (refer to System and Module Start-up and Shut-down Procedures)
  3. Remove the two screws in the keyhole slots that mount the CIB assembly to the upper chassis. (Chassis Interface Board Housing Location).
  4. Remove the CIB from the upper chassis.
