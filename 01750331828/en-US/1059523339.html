Removal

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Perform the module shut-down procedure (refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  3. Remove two pop-in cable tie straps.
  4. Disconnect the touch screen USB adapter cable from the main fascia cable harness.
  5. Pop up the black tabs to release the touch screen controller connectors. Disconnect the three ribbon cables from the touch screen controller (Three Ribbon Cables ).
  6. CAUTION
    Ribbon cables and their connections are fragile. Improper disconnecting or connecting will damage the cable and/or the controller, resulting in improper touch screen operation. Handle with care.
