Removal

  1. Open the upper chassis rear door.
  2. Using T/SOP, perform the system shutdown procedure (Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  3. Extend the cash dispenser upper unit on its slides from the upper chassis.
  4. Disconnect two cables from the upper unit (Cash Dispenser Upper Unit ).
    • Ethernet cable
    • Power cable
  5. Remove the screw that secures the ground strap and disconnect the ground strap.
  6. Remove the two screws that secure the cable retractor to the upper unit (Cash Dispenser Upper Unit ).
  7. Remove the cable retractor from the upper unit.
  8. Remove the two Torx screws that secure the upper unit to the left slide. Do not remove the Allen screw (Cash Dispenser Upper Unit ).
  9. Remove the two Torx screws that secure the upper head unit to the right slide. Do not remove the Allen screw.
  10. Lift the upper unit up, then remove the upper unit from the system.
