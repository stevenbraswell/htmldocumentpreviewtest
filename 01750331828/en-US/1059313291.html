Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the RM4 recycler, CMD-V6C or CMD-V6A cash dispenser shutter.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Using T/SOP, perform the shutdown procedure (refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  3. Rotate the shaft of the shutter to open the shutter (Removing the RM4 Recycler, CMD-V6C or CMD-V6A Cash Dispenser Shutter).
  4. Disconnect the USB cable and the power cable from the shutter.

  8. Open the gate slightly to allow you to pivot the shutter up and off the shutter hooks.
  9. Remove the shutter from the fascia.
