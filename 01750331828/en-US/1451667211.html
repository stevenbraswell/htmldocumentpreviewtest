Removal

Replace the user guide light (UXL) for the form printer or passbook printer if the light is damaged.

Depending on the type and size of the printer bezel will determine how many UXL lights there will be.

  1. Remove the three screws that secure the printer bezel to the filler panel (15-inch Display Filler Panel).
  2. Disconnect the UXL light cable.
  3. Using a flat blade screwdriver, insert the screwdriver in the slots of the printer bezel.
  4. Remove the printer bezel from the filler panel.
  5. Unsnap and remove the LTI back cover from the bezel.
  6. Remove the LED PCBA from the back cover.
