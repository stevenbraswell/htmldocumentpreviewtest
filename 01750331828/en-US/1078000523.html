Removal

  1. Unlock and open the safe door.
  2. Unlatch and completely extend the RM4H recycler lower module on its slide assemblies.
  3. Remove all cassettes from the RM4H recycler lower module (RM4H Cassettes).
  4. Remove the mounting screw and ground cable from the RM4H recycler (Disconnect Cables).
  5. Remove the removable cable ties from the cables.
  6. Disconnect the following cables from the RM4H main controller (Disconnect Cables).
    • X4 safe door switch
    • X3 USB
    • X2 Can-Bus In
    • X1 Power IN 38V

  7. 1

    Ground cable screw

    2

    Ground cable

    3

    Washer

    4

    X4 Safe door switch

    5

    X3 USB

    6

    X2 Can-Bus In

    7

    X1 Power IN 38V

  8. The RM4H lower unit is secured to slide assemblies with seven Torx screws on each side. Refer to Removing the RM4H Recycler from the System).
  9. WARNING

    Make sure not to loosen or remove the mounting screws for the RM4H recycler alignment pins during the following step (see Removing the RM4H Recycler from the System). These pins correctly position and help support the weight of the RM4V recycler. Equipment damage or severe personal injury could result if the alignment pins are loosened or removed.

  10. DO NOT remove the mounting screws (three each side) for the RM4H recycler alignment pins (see Removing the RM4H Recycler from the System).
  11. WARNING

    The RM4H recycler (without cassettes) is heavy. To prevent personnel injury or equipment damage, two people should lift and move the RM4H recycler in the following step.

  12. Lift up and remove the RM4H recycler from the system.

1

Remove the seven Torx screws

2

DO NOT remove these screws
