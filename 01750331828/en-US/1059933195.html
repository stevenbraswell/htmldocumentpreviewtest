Reinstallation

Reverse the order of the steps in Removal to replace the heaters for the encrypting PIN pad.

CAUTION
  1. When reassembling the heaters into the compression brackets and reinstalling them to the fascia, be careful not to pinch the heater cables.
  1. The right side heater is positioned in the upper area of its bracket. The left side heater is positioned in the lower area of its bracket.
