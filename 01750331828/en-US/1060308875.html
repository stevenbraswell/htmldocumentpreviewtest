Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the ActivEdge card reader bezel.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Using T/SOP, remove the system from consumer service and follow the institution's procedures to place the system in the service mode (Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  3. Remove the RM4 recycler or CMD-V6 cash dispenser shutter from the fascia (RM4 Recycler, CMD-V6C or CMD-V6A Cash Dispenser Shutter).
  4. Disconnect the bezel lighting back cover cable from the wiring harness (ActivEdge Bezel).

  5. 1

    Lighting back cover

    2

    Two screws (ActivEdge bezel to the fascia)

    3

    Bezel

    4

    Bezel lighting back cover cable

  6. Remove the two screws that secure the drip tray to the bezel.
  7. Remove the two screws that mount the ActivEdge card reader bezel to the fascia, and remove the bezel.
  8. NOTICE
    The two screws at the top of the bezel are shorter than the two screws at the bottom of the bezel.

  9. Unsnap the lighting back cover from the ActivEdge bezel.
