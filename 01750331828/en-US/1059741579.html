Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the 15-inch consumer display.

NOTICE
The 15-inch consumer display can have either vandal shield or touch screen, but not both.
  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Using the external control unit, perform the shutdown procedure (refer Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  3. See Fan Bracket (on fascia) and remove the two screws and the two nuts to remove the fan bracket from the fascia.

    Fan bracket

    2

    Two fan bracket nuts

    3

    Two fan bracket screws

    4

    Fan cable

  5. Disconnect the right side function keys cable (Function Keys (right side)) from the EPP soft key cable. Retain the function keys to install on the new display screen mounting bracket (Function Keys (right side) for 15-inch Consumer Display).

  6. 1

    Three screws

    2

    Function key shim

    3

    EPP soft key cable

    4

    Function keys with ribbon cable

  7. Remove the three screws from the function key assembly. Remove the function key shim.
  8. CAUTION
    Ribbon cables and their connections are fragile. Improper disconnecting or connecting will damage the cable and/or the controller, resulting in improper touch screen operation. Handle with care.

  9. Pop up the black tabs to release the touch screen controller connectors. Disconnect the three ribbon cables from the touch screen controller (Three Ribbon Cables).
  10. Disconnect the touch screen USB adapter cable from the main fascia cable harness.
  11. Remove the two screws that secure the touch screen controller assembly to the display. There are different holes for either the 15-inch display or the 19-inch display (Three Ribbon Cables).

  12. 1

    Touch screen controller assembly

    2

    Ribbon cable

    3

    Ribbon cable

    4

    Slot in the fascia for the touch screen ribbon cables

    5

    Black tabs on connectors

    6

    Two screws to mount the touch screen controller to the display (15-inch display)

    7

    Two holes to mount the touch screen controller to the display (19-inch display)

  13. NOTICE
    Retain the touch screen controller and the screws for reuse on the replacement display.
  14. If necessary, to replace the touch screen PCBA, remove four screws and disconnect the USB adapter cable. Remove the controller PCBA (Touch Screen Controller).

  15. 1

    Four screws that mount the touch screen controller PCBA

    2

    Monitor power cable

    3

    LCD dimmer sensor cable

    4

    Display port cable

    5

    Touch screen controller PCBA

    6

    Touch screen controller bracket

    7

    Touch screen controller mounting screws

  16. Disconnect the monitor power cable, display port cable and LCD dimmer sensor cable from the display (Touch Screen Controller).
  17. Remove the two screws at the bottom display bracket that secure the bottom bracket to the fascia (Removing the Display).

  18. 1

    Three top screws to the display

    2

    Three top screws to fascia

    3

    Top display bracket

    4

    Display

    5

    Two bottom screws to the display

    6

    Two bottom screws to fascia

    7

    Bottom display bracket

  19. Remove the three screws at the top display bracket that secure the top bracket to the fascia.
  20. Remove the display and brackets from the fascia (Removing the Display).
  21. Disconnect the left function key cable from the EPP soft key cable. Retain the function keys to install on the new display screen mounting bracket (Function Keys (left side) for 15-inch Consumer Display)
  22. Remove the three top screws that secure the top mounting bracket to the display (Removing the Display).
  23. Remove the two bottom screws that secure the bottom mounting bracket to the display.
  24. Remove and retain the display mounting brackets and screws for reuse on the replacement display.
