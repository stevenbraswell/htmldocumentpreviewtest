Fascia Component Removal and Reinstallation

Most fascia component removal and reinstallation procedures are the same for DN Series™ 450 with extended window trim as the standard DN Series™ 450. Refer to Fascia Component Removal and Reinstallation. However, procedures that are unique to the extended window trim system are provided in this Appendix. Refer to DN Series™ 450 Extended Window Trim Components . Some components have been relocated to other areas of the system, even though the removal and reinstallation procedures are the same as standard DN Series™ 450.
