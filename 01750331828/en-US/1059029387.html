Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the dip card reader and bezel.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Perform the T/SOP module shut-down procedure for the dip card reader (refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  3. Remove the RM4 recycler shutter (RM4 Recycler, CMD-V6C or CMD-V6A Cash Dispenser Shutter)
  4. Remove the screw that secures the ground cable and disconnect the ground cable.
  5. Disconnect the following cables from the Dip card reader PCBA (Removing the Dip Card Reader from the Fascia).
    • USB logic cable
    • Power cable
    • UXL light cable
    • Capacitor cable (if present)
  6. Remove the pop-in tie strap that secures the cables.

  8. See Removing the Dip Card Reader from the Fascia and remove the four screws (two at the top, two at the bottom) that mount the dip card reader, cover, and bezel to the fascia.
  9. Remove the tie straps for the drip tray hose.
  10. Remove the two screws that secure the card reader drip tray to the fascia Drip Tray, and remove the drip tray.

  12. See Drip Tray and remove the four screws that mount the Dip card reader bezel to the fascia.
  13. Remove the dip card reader, cover, and bezel from the fascia.
  14. Using a flat blade screw driver, push in on the snap feature and remove the Dip card reader cover from the card reader (Dip Card Reader Cover and Rails ).

  16. Using a flat blade screw driver, release the snap features on the Dip card reader left and right side rails (Dip Card Reader Cover and Rails ). Remove and retain the rails from the card reader. The new Dip card reader may or may not have side rails already attached.
