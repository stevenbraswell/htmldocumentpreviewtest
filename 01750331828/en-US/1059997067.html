Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the identification card scanner.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Perform the T/SOP module shut-down procedure for the identification card scanner (refer to Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  3. Disconnect the USB, power and ground cables from the identification card scanner (Driver License (Identification Card) Scanner).
  4. NOTICE

    Note the orientation of the scanner cable connection ports for proper reinstallation.

  6. Remove the four nuts that secure the identification card scanner bracket to the fascia (Driver License (Identification Card) Scanner).
  7. Remove the identification card scanner from the system.
  8. Remove the two screws that secure the identification card scanner to the bracket.
  9. Remove the scanner from the bracket.
