Starting up the System

NOTICE

After shutting down the system, you must wait at least five minutes before starting up the system again.

  • Gain access to the external control unit (see chapter “Description”, section "System overview").
  • Make sure the power switch is on.
  1. Press the on/off button on the external control unit (see chapter External control unit Overview).
  • The ON/OFF LED lights up green.
  • The system starts.
