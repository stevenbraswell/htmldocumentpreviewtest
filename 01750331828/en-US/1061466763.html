Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the DC/DC 703W power supply.

  1. Disconnect the system power cord from the electrical outlet on the wall.
  2. Open the upper chassis rear door.
  3. Using T/SOP, perform the system shutdown procedure (refer to Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  4. Unlock the latch on the coin module tray and fully extend the tray.

  5. 1

    Power supply cover screws

    2

    Power supply cover

    3

    Power supply

  6. Through the upper chassis, remove the two screws that secure the power supply cover (Access to Power Supply Cover Screws).
  7. Remove the cover from the power supply.
  8. Disconnect three cables (AC/DC power supply, chassis interface board, battery) from the DC/DC power supply (Power Supply Connectors).

  9. 1

    P3 - Battery

    2

    P2 - AC/DC power supply

    3

    Airflow

    4

    P4 - USB (optional)

    5

    P1 - Chassis interface board (CIB)

    6

    Two nuts

  10. Loosen the two nuts that mount the DC/DC power supply to the system (Power Supply Connectors).
  11. Remove the power supply from the system.
