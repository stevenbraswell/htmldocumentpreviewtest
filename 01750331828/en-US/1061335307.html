Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the RM4V recycler.

  1. Unlock and open the safe door.
  2. Perform the system shut-down procedure for the RM4V recycler lower module (refer to Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  3. Unlatch and completely extend the RM4V recycler lower module on its slide assemblies.
  4. Remove all cassettes from the RM4V recycler lower module (RM4V Cassettes ).
  5. Remove the mounting screw and ground cable from the RM4V recycler.
  6. Remove the removable cable ties from the cables.
  7. Disconnect the following cables from the main controller (RM4V Main Controller PCBA).
    • J5 safe door switch
    • J2 USB B
    • J3 Upper comm
    • J1 38V input

  8. 1

    RM4V main controller PCBA

    2

    J5 Safe door switch

    3

    J2 USB B

    4

    J3 Upper comm

    5

    J1 38V Input

  9. WARNING

    Make sure not to loosen or remove the mounting screws for the RM4V recycler alignment pins during the following step (see Removing the RM4V Recycler from the System). These pins correctly position and help support the weight of the RM4V recycler. Equipment damage or severe personal injury could result if the alignment pins are loosened or removed.

  10. Remove the mounting screws (three each side) for the RM4V recycler (see Removing the RM4V Recycler from the System).
  11. WARNING

    The RM4V recycler (without cassettes) is heavy. To prevent personnel injury or equipment damage, two people should lift and move the RM4V recycler in the following step.

  12. Carefully lift the RM4V recycler from its slide assemblies.
