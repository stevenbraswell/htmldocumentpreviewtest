Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the fascia switches. It is recommended to replace both fascia switches. When possible, remove the upper chassis top and side panels (Top Panel and Side Panel) to perform this procedure.

  1. Using T/SOP, place the system in the service mode (Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  2. To remove the fascia switch, unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  3. Remove the side panel of the upper chassis to access the fascia switch (Side Panel).
  4. Remove two screws that secure the left fascia switch bracket to the system (Fascia Switches Locations and Fascia Release Latch Bracket).
  5. Remove one screw that secures the release latch.
  6. Slide the fascia switch bracket assembly up and off of the system.
  7. Remove two screws that secure the fascia switch to the bracket and remove the switch.
  8. Remove the three screws that secure the right fascia switch bracket to the system (Fascia Switches Locations).
  9. Remove the screws that secure the right fascia switch to the bracket.
