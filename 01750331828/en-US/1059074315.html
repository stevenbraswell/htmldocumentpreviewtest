Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the upper chassis mounted ActivEdge card reader.

  1. Open the upper chassis rear door.
  2. Perform the T/SOP module shut-down procedure for the ActivEdge card reader (refer Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up) .
  3. Slide the component tray away from the fascia.
  4. Disconnect the USB and power cables from the ActivEdge card reader (Removing the ActivEdge Card Reader (upper chassis mounted)). If present, also disconnect the regular battery backup cable from the capacitor behind the card reader. There is no ground cable to disconnect.
  5. Remove one screw to remove the card retain bin behind the ActivEdge card reader.
  6. Remove the two screws from underneath the card reader that secure the card reader to the spring sheet on top of the component tray (Removing the ActivEdge Card Reader (upper chassis mounted)).

  9. Remove the ActivEdge card reader from the upper chassis.
