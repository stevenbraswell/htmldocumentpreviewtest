Reinstallation

Perform the following steps to install the extended window trim assembly.

  1. Release the left and right fascia latches and open the fascia (Fascia Latches).
  2. Locate the box that contains the window trim pieces. Lay out the window trim pieces on the floor like a square (Corner Screws). The trim piece mounting brackets are already preassembled to the side trim pieces.
  3. Install two screws at each corner to assemble the trim pieces to each other (Corner Screws).

  9. Using three screws, secure the top trim support bracket, that is preassembled to the top trim piece, to the top of the fascia (Trim Pieces to Adjustment Bracket on Fascia).
