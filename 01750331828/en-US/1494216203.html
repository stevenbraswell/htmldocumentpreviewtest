Removal

  1. Perform the T/SOP module shut-down procedure for the ActivEdge card reader (Modules - Shutting Down and Starting Up).
  2. Unlatch and completely extend the fascia.
  3. Remove the RM4 recycler or CMD-V6 cash dispenser shutter (RM4 Recycler, CMD-V6C or CMD-V6A Cash Dispenser Shutter).
  4. If necessary, remove the tie strap that secures the drip tray drain tube, but the drain tube stays in place.
  5. Disconnect the USB and power cables from the ActivEdge card reader (Removing the ActivEdge Card Reader ). Also disconnect the regular battery backup cable from the capacitor underneath the card reader, if present. There is no ground cable to disconnect.
  6. Remove two screws on the side of the ActivEdge 18° complete bracket that secures the bracket to the center divider of the fascia.
  7. Remove the ActivEdge card reader and the bracket from the fascia.
  8. Remove the two mounting screws that secure the ActivEdge card reader to the ActivEdge 18° complete bracket.
  9. Remove the card reader from the bracket.
