Removal

Perform the following steps to remove the motorized card reader bezel.

  1. Unlock and completely extend the fascia on its slide assemblies.
  2. Using T/SOP, remove the system from consumer service and follow the institution's procedures to place the system in the service mode (Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  3. Remove the RM4 recycler shutter from the fascia (RM4 Recycler, CMD-V6C or CMD-V6A Cash Dispenser Shutter).
  4. Disconnect the user guide light (UXL) from the pig tail connector.
  5. Remove the two screws that secure the drip tray to the bezel (Motorized Card Reader Bezel Screws ).
  6. Remove the three screws that secure the back cover and the bezel to the fascia.
  7. Remove the one remaining screw that mounts the card reader bezel to the fascia.

  10. Unsnap the bezel back cover from the motorized card reader front bezel (Motorized Card Reader Bezel Screws ).
  11. If necessary, unsnap the LED PCBA.
