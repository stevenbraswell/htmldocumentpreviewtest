Removal

  1. Using T/SOP, perform the system shutdown procedure (Starting up / Shutting down the System).
  2. Open the rear upper chassis door.
  3. Extend the recycling module 4 vertical upper unit on its slides from the upper chassis.
  4. Disconnect three cables from the upper unit (Recycling Module, 4 - Vertical Upper Unit (RM4V)).
    • Ethernet cable
    • Power cable
    • USB cable
  5. Remove the screw that secures the ground strap and disconnect the ground strap.
  6. Remove the two screws that secure the cable retractor to the RM4V upper unit (Recycling Module, 4 - Vertical Upper Unit (RM4V)).
  7. Remove the cable retractor from the RM4V upper unit.
  8. Remove the latch to gain access to the screws in the next step (Recycling Module, Generation Four Vertical (RM4V) Latch).
  9. Remove the three Torx screws that secure the upper unit to the left slide. Do not remove the Allen screw (Recycling Module, Generation Four Vertical (RM4V) Upper Unit Latch).
  10. Remove the three Torx screws that secure the upper head unit to the right slide. Do not remove the Allen screw.
  11. Lift the upper unit up, then remove the RM4V upper unit from the system.
